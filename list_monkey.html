Info:

The dart monkey is one of the most dangerous people there are on this planet. He's dangerous because of his speed and mobility. You can recognise him by a specific sound he makes. You can hear this sound by clicking on the button. The dart monkey also has very dangerous projectiles as you can see in the second picture. These projectiles can travel as fast as 120 km/h. He is also the leader of a clan named Super Monkey Fan Club they also travel with darts on hand. If you ever see him run to safety and call local authorities.
