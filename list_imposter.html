This alien is very dangerous because of his ability to pop out of any vent. He can travel thousands of miles in just a second by going out of a vent. It mostly travels alone, but it has been spotted with a companion once in 1928. This alien doen have a very loud vent noise, a sample can be heard in the player. If you ever see him run to safety and call local authorities.
